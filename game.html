Matching game Good luck!

Here are the words in Dutch we teach to be able to play the game

Doei means hi

Goedemorgen means good morning

Alstublieft means please

Dank u wel means Thank You

Tot ziens means see you

Goedenavond means good afternoon

Now all you have to do is to find for each word in English its translation in Dutch (or viceversa) by looking on the other side of the question marks
